Plots Summary

Total Budget Allocation by Scenario

Compares the total budget allocated across different scenarios.

  • Highlights variations in overall allocations across scenarios like Basic, Category Limits, Key Programs, and Combined.
  • Demonstrates how constraints (e.g., department limits, category spending limits) affect total spending.
  • Shows whether stricter constraints result in higher savings or allocations to priority areas.
  • Helps decision-makers choose a scenario based on total expenditure preferences.

Budget Distribution by Category

Displays how budget allocations are distributed across expense categories for each scenario.

  • Shows which categories (e.g., "Personnel Services," "Contractual Services") get prioritized under different scenarios.
  • Highlights how specific constraints (e.g., spending limits per category) shift allocations.
  • Ensures fair and logical allocation across categories.
  • Allows stakeholders to observe changes in category-level priorities for each scenario.

Key Programs Budget Allocation

Focuses on how budget allocations vary for critical programs (e.g., "Mayor's Administration" and "Public Safety") across scenarios.

  • Shows how the scenarios allocate funds to essential programs.
  • Illustrates whether critical programs maintain funding stability under stricter constraints.
  • Provides reassurance that key public services are not adversely affected.
  • Indicates how scenarios address program funding growth compared to previous years.

Scenario Correlation Matrix (Heatmap)

Compares correlations between departmental budget allocations across different scenarios.

  • Identifies similarities and differences in funding patterns across scenarios.
  • Helps stakeholders see which scenarios align more closely in terms of funding distribution.
  • Shows how constraints reshape allocation correlations between programs and departments.
  • Enables planners to identify potential redundancies or opportunities for reallocation.
