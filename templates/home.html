Overview of FY25 Budget

The FY25 Recommended Operating Budget totals $4.64 billion, reflecting an 8% increase over FY24. It represents an additional $344 million investment in City operations.

The Operating Budget supports the day-to-day functions of the City, such as public safety, education, and public health. It also includes mandatory yearly payments for long-term commitments like retirement pensions and debt services related to the Capital Plan.

This tool provides detailed insights into the allocation, efficiency, and analysis of the FY25 budget, allowing you to:

  • Explore department-level budget allocations.
  • Analyze constraints like category spending limits and program-specific priorities.
  • Visualize how changes impact overall expenditure and outcomes.

For more detailed budget data, visit the official dataset: Operating Budget Dataset.

Data Preprocessing: Missing values in the budget data have been successfully filled with the mean value of the corresponding budget rows.
