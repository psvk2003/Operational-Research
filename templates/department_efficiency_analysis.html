Plots Summary

Distribution of Efficiency Scores

A histogram displaying the efficiency scores (FY23 Actual Expense as a percentage of FY24 Appropriation) for departments. Lower scores indicate underutilization, while higher scores indicate overspending.

  • Identifies whether most departments spend within their appropriated budgets.
  • Helps spot departments with significant underutilization or overspending issues.
  • Departments scoring near 100% are likely efficient in utilizing allocated funds.
  • Provides evidence for adjusting future budget appropriations based on efficiency patterns.

Budget Scenarios Comparison

A grouped bar chart comparing budget allocations across three scenarios—Conservative Growth, Aggressive Optimization, and Strategic Investment—for each expense category.

  • Allows stakeholders to evaluate the potential impact of different budgeting strategies.
  • Highlights areas where aggressive cost-cutting can yield significant savings.
  • Demonstrates the benefits of targeted investments in critical areas.
  • Provides a clear visual comparison between current allocations and alternative scenarios.

Department Clusters by Growth and Budget

A scatter plot showing departments grouped into clusters based on growth rate (FY22 to FY25) and budget size using K-means clustering.

  • Cluster_0: High growth rate detected. Consider implementing growth controls.
  • Cluster_1: - High growth rate detected. Consider implementing growth controls. - Large budget allocation. Explore efficiency opportunities.
  • Cluster_2: - High growth rate detected. Consider implementing growth controls.
  • Cluster_3: - Negative growth trend. Review for potential resource constraints.

Expense Category Correlations

A heatmap showing the correlations between expense categories based on department-wise FY25 budget allocations.

  • Identifies relationships between expense categories, such as whether increases in one category align with increases in another.
  • Helps in reallocating funds by understanding which categories have a strong negative correlation.
  • Provides insights into how different expense categories interact, aiding in balanced budget planning.
  • Highlights areas requiring independent analysis due to weak or no correlation.
